# Participant User Guide

## Welcome to PromptLab! 👋

This guide will help you navigate through the experiment interface.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the System
1. Click the link provided by your instructor
2. You'll see the PromptLab login screen
3. If you see an error, try opening in an incognito/private browser window

### Step 2: Login
1. Enter the **last 4 characters of your NRIC**
   - Example: If your NRIC ends with "123A", enter `123A`
   - The system is case-insensitive (123a or 123A both work)
2. Click **"Start Session"**
3. You'll receive a unique participant ID for tracking

## Using the Interface

### Submitting Prompts

#### Text Prompts
1. Type or paste your prompt in the text editor
2. The editor supports:
   - Code syntax highlighting
   - Multiple lines (press Enter for new lines)
   - Copy/paste from other sources
3. Click **"Submit Prompt"** when ready

#### Image Prompts (Optional)
1. Click **"📎 Attach Image"**
2. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, etc.)
3. Add text description if needed
4. Click **"Submit Prompt"**

### AI Responses
- Responses appear below your prompt within 2-5 seconds
- You can copy the response text
- Previous responses remain visible for reference

### Scenarios
- You'll work through multiple scenarios
- Each scenario has a specific task or question
- Some scenarios may auto-advance after submission
- Progress indicator shows completion: "Scenario 2 of 5"

## Demographics Form (One-time)

After completing 3 prompts, you'll see a demographics form:

### Required Information
- ✅ Consent to participate
- ✅ Participant ID (auto-filled)

### Optional Information
- Age group (18-24, 25-34, etc.)
- Gender
- Educational background
- Field of study
- English proficiency
- Coding experience
- AI/LLM usage frequency

**Note**: You can skip optional fields, but providing them helps our research.

## Exit Survey (Final Step)

After all scenarios, you'll see a brief survey about your experience:

### Survey Questions (Scale 0-100)
1. **Mental Demand**: How mentally demanding was the task?
2. **Confidence**: How confident were you in your responses?
3. **AI Reliance**: How much did you rely on the AI?
4. **Overall Experience**: Rate your overall experience

## Tips for Success

### Do's ✅
- **Be specific** in your prompts for better AI responses
- **Use natural language** - write as you would ask a colleague
- **Include context** when relevant
- **Experiment** with different prompt styles
- **Save your work** externally if you want to keep responses

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't refresh the page (you'll lose progress)
- Don't use the back button
- Don't open multiple tabs of the same session
- Don't share your participant ID with others

## Common Issues & Solutions

### "Cannot submit prompt"
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure prompt isn't empty
- Try refreshing the page once

### "AI response not appearing"
- Wait up to 10 seconds
- Check for error messages
- Try submitting again

### "Lost my progress"
- Your responses are saved automatically
- Contact instructor with your participant ID
- You can continue from where you left off

### "Page not loading"
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does this take?**
A: Typically 15-30 minutes depending on scenarios

**Q: Can I take breaks?**
A: Yes, but stay on the same page. Don't close the browser.

**Q: Is there a right answer?**
A: No! We're studying how people interact with AI, not grading responses.

**Q: Can I go back to previous scenarios?**
A: No, the system is designed to move forward only.

**Q: Will my responses be graded?**
A: No, this is for research, not assessment.

**Q: Can I see others' responses?**
A: No, all responses are private and anonymized.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: That's okay! Natural interactions help our research.
